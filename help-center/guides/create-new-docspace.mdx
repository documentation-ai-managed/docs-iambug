---

## Overview
Create a docspace to manage project documents.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    Log in to your iambug account and click "Dashboard".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Docspace'">
    Press the "New Docspace" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up details">
    Enter a name, description, and invite your team members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click "Create" to finish. Start adding docs to your new docspace!
  </Step>
</Steps>
