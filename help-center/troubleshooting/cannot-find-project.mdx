---
title: Cannot Find My Project
description: Steps to fix missing projects in iambug.
---

## Symptoms
- Project not visible on dashboard
- Search returns no results

## Causes
- Insufficient permissions
- Project archived

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check team access">
    Ask an admin to confirm your membership in the project docspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unarchive project">
    Go to "Archived" tab and click "Restore" on your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
