## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account verification
- No prior setup required
</Callout>

## Step-by-Step Setup

Follow these steps to create your iambug account, set up a documentation space, add your first document, and customize it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up for an Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.iambug.com/signup` and enter your email, name, and a secure password.

    Click **Create Account**. iambug sends a verification email—check your inbox and click the link to activate.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Use a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Documentation Space" icon="folder-plus">
    After login, click **New Space** in the dashboard.

    Enter a name like "My Project Docs", select **Public** or **Private** visibility, and choose a slug (e.g., `my-project-docs`).

    Click **Create**. Your space appears in the sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add and Edit Your First Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Document**. Name it "Introduction".

    Use the built-in MDX editor to add content. Here's a starter example:

    <CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,Preview">
    ````markdown
    # Welcome to My Project

    This is your first document in iambug.

    ## Quick Features
    - Easy editing
    - Real-time previews
    - Version history
    ````

    ````markdown
    # Welcome to My Project

    This is your first document in iambug.

    ## Quick Features
    • Easy editing
    • Real-time previews
    • Version history
    ````
    </CodeGroup>

    Click **Publish** to make it live.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Your Space" icon="settings">
    Go to **Space Settings** > **Customization**.

    Set your brand color to `#3B82F6`, upload a logo, and configure the navigation menu.

    Save changes. Your space now reflects your branding.

    <Image
      src="https://via.placeholder.com/800x400/3B82F6/FFFFFF?text=Customized+Dashboard"
      alt="Example of customized iambug documentation space dashboard"
      width="800"
      height="400"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

    ````bash
    curl -X POST https://api.iambug.com/v1/spaces \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
      -d '{"name": "New Space", "slug": "new-space"}'
    ````

    ````javascript
    const response = await fetch('https://api.iambug.com/v1/spaces', {
      method: 'POST',
      headers: {
        'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY',
        'Content-Type': 'application/json',
      },
      body: JSON.stringify({
    ````
